About the Conference

In the era of modern communication and Internet systems, people rely on the Internet for their day-to-day life activities in office, educational institute, business, marketing and others. Moreover, we know that data generated from our daily life activity is also playing a major role in our lifestyle. Every day we generate a huge amount of data which may be our personal data or organizational data. The size of data is tremendously increasing with the increase size of network or Internet, increased number of users in the Internet or due to the wide application areas of modern Internet. In addition, the Gov. of India initiated the Digital India movement and under the umbrella of Digital India many profit and non-profit organizations started reallife projects in various areas such as Agriculture, Healthcare, Transportation, Smart Cities, Power Grid and many others. The fundamental technologies used in most of the applications areas are Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Federated Learning, Internet-of-Things, Blockchain, AI and Software Defined Networking.

The main purpose of the conference entitled “Machine Learning in IoT” is to familiar the stateof-the-art techniques used in Machine Learning (ML) for data analysis and future prediction. The application of ML is very common in the Internet of Things (IoT) network. As IoT network connects billions of smart objects known as Things to the Internet and communication among themselves or to the global network during data sharing, so it generates a huge amount of data in every second. The major concern here is data privacy and security, and also the IoT network security. The ML learning not only plays major role in decision making, future prediction and real-time recommendation but it can also enhance privacy and security to the IoT network. Therefore, we should have a depth knowledge on ML techniques that are quite useful for IoT data analysis, data privacy, and security.

Objectives of the Conference:

  • To make familiar with the state-of-the-art techniques on Machine Learning.
  • To understand the IoT network concepts and applications.
  • To understand the exploratory data analysis techniques.
  • To understand the Time Series Data.
  • Applications of ML in image, text and network data analysis.
  • To understand the concept of feature engineering

Sub-Themes of Conference:

  • Supervised and Unsupervised ML methods
  • Reinforcement learning and use cases
  • Learning from network data
  • To understand the Time Series Data.
  • IoT data capturing and analysis
  • IoT Intrusion detection using ML
